About Bandit

Bandit is an Alusky, or Alaskan Malamute / Siberian Husky mix. He was born in upstate Vermont towards the end of 2019. This fun loving pup lives for his adventures. Whether hiking trails, playing at parks, or visiting family and neighbors, Bandit is always a happy dog. His current adventure is trying to master chasing fish in his pond without getting wet. Good luck, buddy.
